Liam Bates Breadhead SeasonEats Plantminder

About

Inspired by traditional plant journals, Plantminder takes the hassle out of managing your plant's needs. Add your plants info, set up reminders, and start a journal of completed tasks, photos, and notes.

Use the journal to keep track of your plants growth and health. Get reminded when you need to water, mist, or fertilize your plant.
