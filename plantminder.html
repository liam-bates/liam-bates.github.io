About

Inspired by traditional plant journals, PlantMinder takes the hassle out of managing your plant's needs.

- Add your plants and basic information to the app, including photos and notes.

- Get notified when you need to water, mist or fertilize your plant.

- Use the journal to keep track of your plants growth and health.
