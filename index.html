About Me

Hi, I'm Liam! I'm an independent Software Engineer in San Diego, CA focused on application and game development across all Apple platforms (iOS, macOS, tvOS, and watchOS) and technologies. I design, code and build my own apps and games as my main passion and career. I also freelance my skills, so if you are interested in me building or designing you an application or game, please contact me with the details as I am always interested in new projects and would love to hear from you. In addition to app and game development I am also capable in web development (front-end and back-end). Outside of work I'm passionate about hiking, my dog, guitar and board-games.

Code
